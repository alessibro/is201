A brief history of

LSEO

Releases and Tracklists | "Dealbreaker" Music Video | A Random Graph

LSEO is an alternative rock solo project created in 2019 by Alessio Battezzato under the former name Shady Iverson. Battezzato prides himself in writing, recording, and producing all of his music himself as an independent musician.

November of 2019 saw the release of the single “Treehouse”. “Beautiful Brick Wall” was later released in March of 2022 in preparation for the debut EP, “Welcome To The Treehouse”, released in May of that year. “August 30th” was released only 10 days later as the leading single for the second EP, “366 Days Darker”, which release in June of 2022. In July of 2022, LSEO released their third and final EP, “Founders and Females”, featuring the leading tracks “Time To Move On” and “Summer Grace”. In March of 2025, LSEO returned with a new single, “Dealbreaker”.

Despite having 16 tracks released by summer of 2022, Shady Iverson remained quiet for a long time. Songs like “Beautiful Brick Wall”, “Summer Grace”, and “Time To Move On” were performed on occasion at venues like the Green House and the Velour, but very rarely, and usually through Battezzato’s band Bann Woch. Shortly before the release of “Dealbreaker”, Shady Iverson was rebranded to LSEO and by the summer of 2025, live musicians Sam (bass), Chase (guitar), Wyatt (drums), and Jake (guitar) joined. Their first live show was at the Lanai on July 29, 2025.
